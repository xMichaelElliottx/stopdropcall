The Three Steps

Simple steps that save people.

Our name is also our method. Three words that can protect you from losing your savings — or your sense of security.

🛑

Step 1 — Stop

The moment something feels off — an unexpected call, a message asking for money or personal details — stop. Don't act. Don't transfer. Don't click. Scammers thrive on urgency. Pause is your superpower.

📵

Step 2 — Drop

Put down the phone. Close the browser. Step away from the pressure. You are always allowed to end a call, even if it feels rude. A real bank, government agency, or family member will understand.

📞

Step 3 — Call

Call our free AI Voice Hotline. Available around the clock, it will walk you through what just happened, help you decide if it was a scam, and tell you exactly what to do next — in plain, calm English.

Our Mission

Why we exist.

"A future where no senior loses their savings, dignity, or sense of safety to a scammer — because they always had someone to call."

Stop, Drop, Call is an Australian not-for-profit organisation. We believe that every older Australian deserves access to free, accessible, and immediately available protection from financial scams. Through free community seminars and our 24/7 AI Voice Hotline, we put that protection directly in your hands — or rather, at your fingertips.
